About Us - Our Story

Davenport House Clinic has served Stalybridge since 2008, combining clinical expertise with a genuinely local, patient-first approach.

Our Story

Built from a shared vision for better local care

Davenport House Clinic was founded by Tony Gavin and Becky Gavin in 2008 with a clear goal: to create a private physiotherapy and podiatry clinic in Becky’s hometown of Stalybridge that patients could trust.

The clinic is based in a Victorian villa originally built in 1858. Its spacious, characterful treatment rooms gave us the perfect setting to build a practice that reflects our standards of professionalism, warmth, and clinical excellence.

Over time, the clinic has grown through recommendations from local patients across Stalybridge and Tameside. We remain committed to delivering personalised treatment with outstanding service from first enquiry to recovery.

We believe in putting patients first and creating a calm, supportive environment where people can recover with confidence.
