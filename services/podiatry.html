What We Treat

Comprehensive podiatry for common and complex foot concerns

Our podiatry service supports patients from Stalybridge, Tameside, and nearby areas who need private assessment and treatment for pain, skin and nail conditions, and mobility-related foot issues.

  • Heel pain and plantar fasciitis
  • Ingrown toenails and nail management
  • Corns, callus, and cracked heels
  • Fungal nail and skin concerns
  • Bunion-related discomfort
  • Diabetic and high-risk foot care support

Tools and approach

Each appointment starts with a structured clinical assessment and clear explanation of your options.

  • Routine podiatry treatment and preventative care
  • Nail care and conservative ingrown nail management
  • Pressure reduction and footwear guidance
  • Biomechanical screening where appropriate
  • Referral guidance when further medical support is needed

Patient education micro-guides

Daily foot checks

Inspect skin and nails regularly, especially if you have diabetes or reduced sensation.

Footwear matters

Choose supportive shoes with enough toe room and avoid prolonged pressure on painful areas.

Early treatment helps

Persistent pain, recurring nail problems, or skin changes should be assessed early to prevent escalation.

What is the difference between podiatry and chiropody?

The terms are often used interchangeably in UK private practice. Both involve diagnosis and treatment of foot and lower-limb conditions.

Can I book podiatry near Stalybridge without referral?

Yes, most private podiatry appointments can be booked directly.

How often should I attend routine appointments?

Frequency depends on your foot health needs. Your podiatrist will recommend an interval after assessment.
