Common Conditions We Treat

Quick educational guides to common issues seen by our physiotherapy, podiatry, and sports massage teams in Stalybridge.

Condition Library

Musculoskeletal, foot, joint, and sports injury support

If you are unsure which service to book, use these examples as a starting point. Our clinicians will confirm the right treatment pathway at your appointment.

Back Pain

Lower or upper back discomfort linked to posture, lifting, repetitive strain, or acute flare-ups.

Neck Pain & Tension

Stiffness, reduced movement, and pain often associated with desk work, stress, or overuse.

Sciatica

Radiating leg pain associated with nerve irritation in the lower back region.

Shoulder Impingement

Pain and restriction when lifting the arm, often linked to tendon and movement issues.

Tennis or Golfer's Elbow

Elbow pain related to repetitive gripping, lifting, or sport-specific loading.

Knee Pain

Includes overuse discomfort, tracking issues, and pain during stairs, running, or prolonged sitting.

Plantar Fasciitis

Common heel pain, often worse in the morning or after periods of rest.

Achilles Tendon Pain

Posterior ankle pain linked to load, activity progression, footwear, or biomechanical factors.

Ingrown Toenails

Painful nail edge irritation with redness and swelling that may need podiatry treatment.

Corns & Callus

Pressure-related skin thickening causing discomfort in walking or standing.

Sports Muscle Strain

Acute or recurring muscle injuries managed with rehab, load guidance, and soft tissue care.

Postural Overload

Neck, upper back, and hip stiffness from prolonged sitting, driving, or repetitive work tasks.

When to seek help

  • Pain lasting longer than 1-2 weeks
  • Symptoms affecting work, sleep, or activity
  • Recurring foot problems or nail issues
  • Injury recovery not progressing as expected
